Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my first transaction?

Click on "Add" in the navigation menu, fill in the transaction details (type, amount, category, date, and notes), and click "Add Transaction". Your transaction will be immediately visible on your dashboard.

How do I set savings goals?

Go to the "Savings" page from the navigation menu. Click "Set Goal" and enter your target amount and monthly savings target. TrackFlow will show your progress towards achieving your goal.

Can I edit or delete transactions?

Yes! Go to the "History" page to view all your transactions. Each transaction has "Edit" and "Delete" buttons. Click "Edit" to modify details or "Delete" to remove the transaction.

How do I view my financial analytics?

Navigate to the "Analytics" page to see beautiful charts showing your income vs expenses, monthly trends, category breakdown, and AI-powered insights about your spending patterns.

What are AI predictions?

TrackFlow uses machine learning to analyze your spending patterns and predict future expenses. This helps you plan better and make informed financial decisions.
